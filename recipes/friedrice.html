Fried Rice

Description

Fried rice is a dish of cooked rice that has been stir-fried in a wok or a frying pan and is usually mixed with other ingredients such as eggs, vegetables, seafood, or meat.

Ingredients

The ingredients listed below are for making kimchi fried rice.

  • 3 bowls of steamed rice
  • 1 cup of chopped kimchi
  • 1/4 cup of kimchi juice
  • 6 oz of spam, diced
  • 1 egg
  • 1/2 onion, diced
  • 1 green onion, chopped
  • 1 tablespoon of vegetable oil
  • 1 tablespoon of sesame oil
  • 1 tablespoon of sesame seeds

Preparation

  1. Heat up a pan and add the vegetable oil.
  2. Add the onions and cook until they start to brown.
  3. Add the kimchi and spam. Continue frying for 1-2 minutes.
  4. Remove the contents onto a plate and add an egg into the pan.
  5. Break up the egg and cook for 1 minute. Then add the rice, kimchi juice, and water into the pan. Stir fry for about 5 minutes.
  6. Add the previous contents into the pan and mix thoroughly.
  7. Add sesame oil and turn off the heat.
  8. Sprinkle in the green onions and sesame seeds.
  9. Enjoy!
